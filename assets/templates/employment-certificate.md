# 在职证明模板 (Employment Certificate)

## 使用说明
- 打印在公司抬头纸上
- 必须盖公章 + 负责人签字
- 英文版适用所有国家，部分国家（如日本）中英文都需要

---

## 英文版 (English Version)

```
[DATE]

To: Embassy/Consulate General of {{COUNTRY}}
     Visa Section

Dear Sir/Madam,

This is to certify that {{NAME}} (Passport No: {{PASSPORT_NO}}, Date of Birth: {{DOB}}) has been employed by {{COMPANY_NAME}} since {{JOIN_DATE}}. {{HE/SHE}} currently holds the position of {{POSITION}} with a monthly salary of RMB {{SALARY}} (after tax).

{{NAME}} has been granted leave from {{LEAVE_START}} to {{LEAVE_END}} ({{LEAVE_DAYS}} days) for a tourism trip to {{DESTINATION}}. We confirm that {{HE/SHE}} will resume {{HIS/HER}} duties upon {{HIS/HER}} return to China on {{RETURN_DATE}}.

All travel expenses during this trip will be borne by {{HIMSELF/HERSELF}}.

Your kind consideration of {{HIS/HER}} visa application is highly appreciated. Should you require any further information, please do not hesitate to contact us.

Sincerely,

Signature: _____________________
Name: {{HR_NAME}}
Title: {{HR_TITLE}}
Company: {{COMPANY_NAME}}
Address: {{COMPANY_ADDRESS}}
Tel: {{COMPANY_PHONE}}
Date: {{DATE}}

(Company Seal)
```

## 中文版 (Chinese Version — Japan/Korea often require)

```
在职证明

致 {{COUNTRY}} 驻华使领馆：

兹证明 {{NAME}}（护照号：{{PASSPORT_NO}}，出生日期：{{DOB}}）自 {{JOIN_DATE}} 起在我公司担任 {{POSITION}} 一职，月薪为税前人民币 {{SALARY}} 元。

{{NAME}} 拟于 {{LEAVE_START}} 至 {{LEAVE_END}} 前往 {{DESTINATION}} 旅游，我公司已批准其休假申请。{{HE/SHE}} 将于 {{RETURN_DATE}} 返回中国并继续在我司工作。

此次旅行的所有费用由{{HIM/HERSELF}}承担。

特此证明。

{{COMPANY_NAME}}
日期：{{DATE}}

负责人签字：_______________
职务：__________________
电话：__________________
地址：__________________

（加盖公章）
```
