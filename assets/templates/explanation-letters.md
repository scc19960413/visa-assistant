# 解释信模板合集 (Explanation Letters)

签证申请中最常见的解释信场景。所有解释信必须是英文（或英文+中文对照），语气正式且诚实。

---

## 1. 资金来源解释信 (Source of Funds)

适用于：银行流水有大额进账、余额突然增加、自由职业收入不稳定

```
[DATE]

To: Visa Officer
     Embassy of {{COUNTRY}}

Dear Visa Officer,

I am writing to explain the source of funds in my bank account (Account No: {{ACCOUNT_NUMBER}}).

{{EXPLANATION_TEXT}}

For example:
- "The deposit of RMB {{AMOUNT}} on {{DATE}} was a year-end bonus from my employer, as shown in the attached payslip."
- "I have been accumulating savings for this trip over the past {{PERIOD}}. My monthly income is sufficient to cover all travel expenses."
- "The transfer of RMB {{AMOUNT}} on {{DATE}} was from my family member {{NAME}} as a gift to support my travel."

Supporting documents attached:
1. {{DOCUMENT_1}}
2. {{DOCUMENT_2}}

I trust this clarifies the matter. Thank you for your consideration.

Sincerely,
{{NAME}}
Passport: {{PASSPORT_NO}}
Phone: {{PHONE}}
Date: {{DATE}}
```

## 2. 自由职业收入说明信 (Freelance Income Statement)

适用于：自由职业者、个体户、无固定工资流水的申请人

```
[DATE]

To: Visa Officer
     Embassy of {{COUNTRY}}

Dear Visa Officer,

I am {{NAME}}, a self-employed {{PROFESSION}} based in {{CITY}}, China. I am writing to explain my source of income as I do not have a traditional employer.

Since {{START_DATE}}, I have been working as a freelance {{PROFESSION}}, providing services in {{SERVICES}}. My primary clients include:
- {{CLIENT_1}} — Average monthly income: RMB {{AMOUNT_1}}
- {{CLIENT_2}} — Average monthly income: RMB {{AMOUNT_2}}

My average monthly income over the past 12 months is approximately RMB {{AVERAGE_INCOME}}, as evidenced by:
1. Bank statements showing regular income deposits
2. Tax payment records
3. Client contracts / invoices (selected samples attached)

I have sufficient savings for this trip to {{DESTINATION}}. Additionally, I own {{PROPERTY}} and have family ties in {{CITY}}, which ensure my return to China.

Thank you for your kind consideration.

Sincerely,
{{NAME}}
Passport: {{PASSPORT_NO}}
Phone: {{PHONE}}
Date: {{DATE}}
```

## 3. 拒签后重新申请解释信 (Reapplication After Refusal)

适用于：曾被拒签，现在重新申请

```
[DATE]

To: Visa Officer
     Embassy of {{COUNTRY}}

Dear Visa Officer,

I am {{NAME}}. I am reapplying for a {{VISA_TYPE}} visa after my previous application (Ref: {{REF_NO}}) was refused on {{REFUSAL_DATE}}.

I understand the previous refusal was due to {{REFUSAL_REASON}}. Since then, my circumstances have changed:

1. {{CHANGE_1}}
   Example: "I have traveled to {{COUNTRIES}} since the refusal, demonstrating my compliance with visa regulations."

2. {{CHANGE_2}}
   Example: "I have purchased a property in {{CITY}}, strengthening my ties to China."

3. {{CHANGE_3}}
   Example: "I now have {{YEARS}} years of stable employment at {{COMPANY}} with an increased salary."

I am genuinely traveling for tourism purposes only. My detailed itinerary and supporting documents for this trip are attached. I have every intention of returning to China by {{RETURN_DATE}}.

I respectfully request your favorable reconsideration.

Sincerely,
{{NAME}}
Passport: {{PASSPORT_NO}}
Phone: {{PHONE}}
Date: {{DATE}}
```

## 4. 出行目的说明信 (Purpose of Travel)

适用于：复杂行程、多国连游、特殊目的

```
[DATE]

To: Visa Officer
     Embassy of {{COUNTRY}}

Dear Visa Officer,

I am writing to explain the purpose and plan of my upcoming trip to {{DESTINATION}}.

**Trip Summary**
- Dates: {{START_DATE}} to {{END_DATE}} ({{DAYS}} days)
- Purpose: Tourism
- Travel companion(s): {{COMPANIONS}}

**Why This Trip**
{{WHY_PARAGRAPH}}
Example: "I have been fascinated by {{COUNTRY}}'s culture and history for many years. This trip coincides with my National Day holiday, which is the longest break I have during the year."

**Planned Activities**
- {{ACTIVITY_1}}
- {{ACTIVITY_2}}
- {{ACTIVITY_3}}

**After the Trip**
I will return to {{CITY}}, China on {{RETURN_DATE}} to resume my duties at {{COMPANY}}. My return flight {{FLIGHT_NO}} is booked for {{RETURN_DATE}}.

Thank you for considering my application.

Sincerely,
{{NAME}}
Passport: {{PASSPORT_NO}}
Phone: {{PHONE}}
Date: {{DATE}}
```
