# 邀请函模板 (Invitation Letter)

适用于：探亲访友签证（申根、美国B2、英国、加拿大等）

---

## 英文邀请函 (For Schengen / US / UK / Canada / Australia)

```
[DATE]

To: Visa Officer
     Embassy/Consulate of {{COUNTRY}}

Subject: Invitation Letter for {{GUEST_NAME}}

Dear Sir/Madam,

I, {{HOST_NAME}}, holder of {{HOST_NATIONALITY}} passport (No: {{HOST_PASSPORT_NO}}), currently residing at:

    {{HOST_ADDRESS}}

hereby invite the following person to visit me in {{COUNTRY}}:

**Guest Information**
- Full Name: {{GUEST_NAME}}
- Date of Birth: {{GUEST_DOB}}
- Passport No: {{GUEST_PASSPORT_NO}}
- Relationship to Host: {{RELATIONSHIP}}
- Address in China: {{GUEST_ADDRESS_CHINA}}

**Visit Details**
- Purpose: {{PURPOSE}} (e.g., Family visit / Tourism / Attending wedding)
- Planned Arrival: {{ARRIVAL_DATE}}
- Planned Departure: {{DEPARTURE_DATE}}
- Duration: {{DURATION}} days
- Accommodation: {{ACCOMMODATION_DETAIL}}

{{ACCOMMODATION_OPTIONS:
Option A (staying with host):
"The guest will stay at my residence at the above address. I will provide free accommodation during {{HIS/HER}} visit."

Option B (hotel):
"The guest has booked hotel accommodation as detailed in the attached reservation."}}

I will ensure that my guest complies with the immigration laws of {{COUNTRY}} and returns to China before the visa expires.

**Host Contact Information**
- Phone: {{HOST_PHONE}}
- Email: {{HOST_EMAIL}}

**Documents Attached from Host**
1. Copy of host's passport / residence permit
2. Proof of host's address (utility bill / rental contract)
3. {{ADDITIONAL_HOST_DOCS}}

Should you require any further information, please contact me at the above details.

Sincerely,

_________________________
{{HOST_NAME}}
Date: {{DATE}}
```

---

## 法文邀请函 (For France Schengen — Attestation d'Accueil)

如果需要法国市政厅出具的正式接待证明（Attestation d'Accueil），这是法国特有的要求。邀请人必须在法国的市政厅（Mairie）申请，签证申请时提供原件。

如果是一般酒店住宿，则不需要此证明。此模板仅适用于住在法国朋友/家中的情况。

---

## 注意事项

1. 邀请函+邀请人身份证明（护照/居留卡复印件）必须一起提交
2. 申根探亲：建议邀请人提供最近3个月的水电账单或工资单作为地址+财力证明
3. 美国B2探亲：邀请函不是必需材料，但有的话有帮助
4. 英国/加拿大/澳大利亚：邀请函+邀请人财力证明很重要
5. 关系证明（如亲属关系公证书、合影照片等）可以辅助佐证
